Lobster Roll

Description

Have you been craving an authentic Maine Lobster Roll but aren't able to take a trip to the east coast rigth now? This easy to make lobster roll recipe will blow you away!

This budget friendly recipe allows you to experience the flovor of a delicous Maine Lobster Roll at any time with your family!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds cooked and cubed lobster meat
  • 2 stalks celery, finely chopped
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise (such as Hellman'sÂ®)
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 6 hoagie rolls
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Combine lobster meat, celery, mayonnaise, lemon juice, and chives; mix well. Season with salt and pepper. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Slice sides off hoagie rolls and slice down the center. Brush melted butter on the sides and in the center of rolls and place on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake rolls in the preheated oven for 7 minutes. Stuff with chilled lobster mixture and serve.
